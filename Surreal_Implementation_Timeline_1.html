Key milestones by week
Pre-commencement
Workshop ✓ 6 May
Half-day workshop delivered. Specialist package development, SocialTap in the client journey, and digital confidence. Completed before six-week programme begins.
Week 1
Platform foundation
Timely configured in full. Zapier connected to Vish and Linktree. NZHA business entry commenced. Michelle provides sales figures and supporting content.
Week 2
Automations and survey
Client journey automations configured. Staff survey distributed and responses collected. HTML email templates built. Stylist NZHA intake surveys distributed.
Week 3
Database and directory
Staff database built from survey responses. Specialist directory produced. Website bios updated on Squarespace. Stylist NZHA surveys returned by end of week.
Week 4
Review and SOPs
Phase 1 review and sign-off with Michelle. SOPs commenced. NZHA individual entries being written in parallel. Marketing configuration work begins.
Week 5
Marketing and SocialTap
Klaviyo configured and connected. Email templates built. SocialTap dots installed and mapped. Conversion tracking live. Technology references confirmed for awards entry.
Week 6
Completion and submission
Re-engagement campaign tested. SOPs and onboarding pack issued. All NZHA entries finalised and submitted by 1 July 2026 deadline. Invoice 2 due 8 June.
